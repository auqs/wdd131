Rate My Password

    About

    The purpose of this website is to provide a rating on your password. The website will use a meter of strength to see whether your password is strong or not. There are about 5 levels. Strong, Good, Developing, Okay, Bad. These categories will dictate whether the password has sufficent characters, unique symbols, uppercase and lowercase letters, including numbers.

    Our goal of this website is not to only rate how strong or weak your password is. We also want to educate you what makes a very strong password. We want you to make better choices when creating a password. A password is an important part of our lives, this is how we can stay secure from threat actors that attempt to login into your account.

    Privacy

    We would like to mention that anything inputed are transmitted locally in your browser. Your passwords are never stored, logged, nor transmitted whatsoever!! You can test your password's strength with the utmost assurance that you're protected.
